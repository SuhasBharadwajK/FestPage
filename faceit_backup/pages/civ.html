Cubo Casting
Rules:
  • Team size should not exceed 2.
  • Each team should cast 2 cubes of size(10x10x10).
  • Casting should be done on the date of the event, 9/10/2015, in the college.
  • Your sample will be tested on 10/10/2015 after curing by acceleratory curing tank.
  • Testing will be done by digital compression testing machine and rebound hammer non-destructive testing.
  • Make your mix design to attain the following properties:
    • Target strength.
    • Economic design.
    • Surface finishing of cube.
Material and equipment provided by the college:
  • Cement of grade M53.
  • Aggregates of 12-pass, 10-retain and river sand
  • 10x10x10 cm moulds.
  • Tamping rod and vibrating machine.
  • Acceleratory curing tank (to attain strength of 28 days in 4 hrs of curing in controlled conditions).
